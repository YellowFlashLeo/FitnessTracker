Fitness Tracker

Main idea of the application is to provide convenient, maintainable and simple in daily usage solution for bodybuilders. Every bodybuilder needs to make records of his/her trainings including exercises along with amount of sets/reps and weights used. Application also provides intelligent calories calculator based on food consumed by user of the application. At the moment, it is more like a training journal but in future I would like to add some smart progress tips and trainings.
